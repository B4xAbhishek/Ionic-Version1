How to use the app?

In this app a user will enter all the symptoms he/she is currently facing
The outcome of the app will be dashboard showing the current situation of the user's
local healthcare facilities and ralated vacancies. It will also show a forecast for the
vacancies and treatments available for various illness. A probable state of the person
will also be displayed based on which, a user can decide the urgency of the occuring symptoms

The administrator access and data modification access will be given to
the hospital and medical health planners who will update the vacancies based on which various forecast will be shown
